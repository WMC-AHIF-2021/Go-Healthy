About Us

We are three students of the school HTBLA Leonding and this is our one-year project. Together, we decided to do a sport website. Our idea was to give you the perfect mix of exercises for your whole body. It doesn't matter, if you are at home or anywhere else, because we chose some easy, but also useful, exercises you can do where ever you are. A little extra, that we added to our website, is a calorien calculator. It helps you to count your calories that you should take to yourself per day.
