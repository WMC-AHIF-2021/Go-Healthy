  2. After youu jump, make sure you bend your knees on the landing,too. his will help you to land safely without jarring your joints.
  3. A mistake is to stand up straight and don#t rush your Reps.
  4. Repeat it 2 Sets - 10 Reps/Set

This exercises is good for beginners.

Bulgarian Split Squats

  1. Stand roughly two feet in front of a sturdy bench or chair.
  2. Pick up your right foot and place it on the bench behind you.Your back foot is just there to help you stay balanced.
  3. Try to keep the load balanced evenly across your left foot as you lower downward.
  4. Inhale through this downward phase, then press back to standing by pushing through your left foot and using your left quad and glute to power the upward phase.
  5. Repeat it 2 Sets - 10 Reps/Set

A comman mistake is rising onto the toes, it will make this exercise even harder.

Single - Leg Hip Thurst

  1. Lie faceup with left foot resting on seat chair.
  2. Right leg extended toward ceiling.
  3. Push through left heel to drive hips off the floor.
  4. Pause at the top.
  5. Then slowly lower butt toward mat/towel.
  6. Repeat it 4 Sets - 8 Reps/Set

You can only do this exercise with a chair

Inner Leg Raise

  1. reach your top hip down so your pelvis is level
  2. your waist should pull away from the floor a bit
  3. Maintain this position as you do the movements below
  4. Straight-up lifts: As you exhale, lift your bottom leg, and inhale as you lower it back down without letting it touch the floor
  5. Repeat it 3 Sets - 15 Reps/Set

Make sure you wait a minute before you do the next set.

Jump Rope

  1. First, choose a skipping rope that suits you.
  2. Then you stand broad-legged and jump with the rope in your hands.
  3. Repeat it 3 Sets - 30 jumps

Make sure you choose a rope with the correct lenght.

Goblet Squat

  1. Hold the dumbbell by the weight (not the handle) in a vertical position in front of your chest.
  2. Space your feet between hip- and shoulder-width apart.
  3. Hold your elbows in close to your sides, keep your back straight, and engage your core.
  4. Lower down into the squat position in a controlled motion until your hips are below your knees. Your elbows should track inside your knees, not on the outside.
  5. Hold for a second or two before you push up through your heels to return to the starting position
  6. Repeat it 3 Sets - 15 Reps/Set

Be sure to squeeze your glutes hard when you get back up to the top.
