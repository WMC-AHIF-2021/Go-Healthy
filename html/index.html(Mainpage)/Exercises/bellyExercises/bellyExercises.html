Belly Exercises

Sit Up

  1. Start by lying on your back with your knees bent.
  2. Put your fingertips on the back of your ears.
  3. Lift your torso up as close to your thighs as possible.
  4. Lower your torso down to the floor so you are back in the starting position
  5. Do 3 sets of 10-15 reps.

Russian Twist

  1. Place a mat or towel on the floor. Elevate your upper body.
  2. Twist. With your arms extended in front of you, twist your torso to the right side. Twist until your arms are parallel to the floor. Hold the position for a second or two and breathe out.
  3. Try to do 3 sets of 8-12 reps.

Crunches

  1. Lie on your back on an exercise mat.
  2. Bend your knees so your feet are flat on the floor.
  3. Cross your arms behind your head.
  4. Lift your shoulder blades off of the mat with a smooth, controlled motion.
  5. If the exercise is too easy pull your legs to your chest at the same time.
  6. Do 5 sets 45 seconds

Hollow Hold

  1. Lie on your back, with your arms and legs straight out.
  2. Crunch your abdominal muscles inward, as if you were trying to suck your belly button to the floor.
  3. Raise your arms, legs, and upper back off the floor, creating a “smiley face” with your body.
  4. If the movement is too difficult or you start to feel it in your lower back, bend the legs to make it easier.
  5. Do 3 sets 20 - 30 seconds

Dolphin Plank

  1. Get on your hands and knees.
  2. Raise into dolphin pose.
  3. Walk your feet backward.
  4. Breathe through the position.
  5. Do 3 sets of 20 reps. Take a 30-60 second break before moving on to the next exercise.
