Stretch Exercises

First strechting exercise

  1. At the beginning they stand shoulder-width and stretch their arms to the left and right.
  2. Then they alternately touch the left foot with the right hand and the right foot with the left hand.
  3. Repeat this process 20 times.

It is important that they do not bend their feet and do not go to their knees.

Second stretching exercise

  1. Stand on both legs.
  2. Now try to touch the tips of your feet with your hands.
  3. Make sure that your feet remain stretched out.
  4. Repeat this process 15 times.

Third strechting exercise

  1. Sit down and stretch your left foot away to the right..
  2. Then they try to touch the feet with their hands without bending their legs.
  3. After half a minute they change sides.
  4. Repeat this process 5 times.

Fourth stretching exercise

  1. Stand on one foot and support themselves with one hand on a wall.
  2. Now take the right foot with her right hand and try to pull it as far as possible to her back.
  3. After one minute switch the side.
  4. Repeat this process 4 times.
